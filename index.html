About me

In every job or new project, that I want to start, the most important for me is, that I can use my creativity. In the younger age I created music and lyrics. I had my own studio at home this time. Then in my life came sport. I tried a lot of types of sports like Judo, and some sort of fight trainings and now I go to gym regularly over 6 years. Also I worked in Finland. I started as a casual worker, but for the end of the year I was a foreman and had a team of 15 people. In 2017 I started to trading with crypto currencies. After that I went to the Chicago stock in “Future market place”. Thanks to trading I first thought about programming, because I wanted to create my own robot who trade with algorithms that I will create for him. Then I decided to go learn programming. My inspiration in programming is that I want to be firstly aware about the new technologies and new destinations in programming and design.
